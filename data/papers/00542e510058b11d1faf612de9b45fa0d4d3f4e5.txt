Title: Saturation time of exposure interval for cross-neutralization response to SARS-CoV-2: Implications for vaccine dose interval
Venue: iScience
Year: 2023
Abstract: None
CMU authors: Shinji Watanabe
Other authors: Sho Miyamoto, Y. Kuroda, T. Kanno, A. Ueno, N. Shiwa-Sudo, N. Iwata-Yoshikawa, Yusuke Sakai, N. Nagata, T. Arashiro, A. Ainai, Saya Moriyama, N. Kishida, K. Nojima, Y. Seki, T. Mizukami, H. Hasegawa, H. Ebihara, S. Fukushi, Yoshimasa Takahashi, Maeda Ken, Tadaki Suzuki
tldr: The results highlight the importance of vaccine dosage intervals of 4 months or longer, regardless of the antigenicity of the exposed antigen, to maximize the breadth of serum cross-neutralization covering SARS-CoV-2 Omicron lineages.
